## Description

- Computes the greatest common divisor (GCD) of two non-negative integers using Euclid's algorithm.
-
- The algorithm repeatedly replaces the larger number by its remainder when divided by the smaller
- until the remainder is zero; the non-zero value at that point is the GCD.
